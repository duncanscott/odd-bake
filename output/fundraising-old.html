Rinchen Terzod

Thanks to the Venerable Gyatrul Rinpoche and his students and supporters, we have a fully endowed dharma center and are able to offer a wide range of Dharma teachings, meditation classes and practice opportunities.

In just a short time Gyatrul Rinpoche, and other great Tibetan masters, have planted the seeds and laid out the path to enlightenment. They have given the gift of the Dharma to us, their disciples who live in America.

As we witness the migration of the Dharma to new countries, it is our opportunity and duty to continue this wisdom heritage, bring our practice to fruition and serve the needs of beings, as our lineage masters have done before us. So please help us preserve and propagate the dharma in this great, sacred place of practice, Orgyen Dorje Den, with your offerings of financial support, service and practice.

Orgyen Dorje Den is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) tax-exempt corporation in the State of California. Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Thank you very much,

Board of Directors, Orgyen Dorje Den
